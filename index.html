Meet Dr. Nafla Hisham

Founder & Chief Formulator

Dr. Nafla Hisham is a visionary Ayurvedic physician and cosmetologist who represents the future of integrative beauty and wellness—where time-tested Ayurvedic wisdom meets modern cosmetic innovation.

In 2022, driven by a mission to offer safe, authentic, and results-oriented alternatives to chemical-based cosmetics, she founded ELAT NATURALS, a premium herbal and Ayurvedic cosmetics brand. What started as a passion project has rapidly evolved into a globally trusted wellness label.

  • Knowledge of safety, efficacy and quality standards in cosmetics
